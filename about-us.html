Rang-e-Ishq is organized as a sole proprietorship under the leadership of Maryam Ihtisham, the founder and CEO. The brand operates as part of her entrepreneurial vision and passion for blending traditional Indo-Pak culture with modern sustainable fashion practices

Our mission is to blend Indo-Pak cultural heritage with modern designs, offering unique and high-quality clothing that celebrates love and tradition. We are committed to creating outfits that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also sustainable, ensuring an ethical production process that supports the environment and local artisans.

“A colorful blend of love, tradition, and sustainability”

Welcome to Rang-e-Ishq Store, your premier destination for fashion-forward clothing and accessories. We pride ourselves on offering a curated selection of rare and beautiful items sourced both locally and globally. Our mission is to bring you the latest trends and timeless styles, ensuring every piece reflects quality and elegance. Discover the perfect addition to your wardrobe at Rang-e-Ishq Store.
